About Us

What we do

We Inform

(Customer Education)

Some people want to be equiped with the latest tools and technologies while trying to save money at the same time. We educate our visitors the proper way of acquiring software products and their corresponding licenses so that customers get what they need and proper credit is given to the developers and vendors who spent resources to made those needs satisfied.

We Sell

(Authorized Partner)

We make profit on user purchases by offering them the right variant of software product (operating system, productivity, security, tools or game) slightly marked up yet affordable. We partner with vendors and developers so that we can help them promote their software and get them buying and returnings customers.

We Amuse

(Value Added Reseller)

We make customers enjoy their licensed product for as long as their chosen usage license permits (perpetual, monthly/annual subscription, etc.). Plus, our support team is always ready incase of any kind of trouble. We can assist from the moment a customer made a purchase. With client's permission, we can initiate a remote desktop session so that we can make the installation, activation or troubleshooting as they watch. We don't just sell, we provide value beyond the original order fulfillment.
